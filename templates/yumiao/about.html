About Us

Me Meet Cat

The objective of our application is to give people an opportunity to post message about stray cats to be adopted, adopt cats online and share opinions about raising a cat. Users could view all information about cat to be adopted. They also could search cats with age, breed and location. Those who are intended to adopt cats could upload personal information and reason for adopting to get an opportunity. Cat lovers will be able to communicate at the comment zone. Everyone who have questions about raising cats could post their questions at comment zone.
